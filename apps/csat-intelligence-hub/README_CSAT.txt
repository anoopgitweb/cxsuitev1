CSAT Intelligence Hub
=====================

This is a separate CSAT app created beside the NPS Analyzer.

Folder inside this portable package:
apps\csat-intelligence-hub

Notes:
- The original NPS Analyzer folder is not modified.
- The older CSAT folders are also left untouched.
- The app is opened from the CX Intelligence Suite menu as CSAT Intelligence Hub.
- CSAT mode supports common 1-5, 1-7, and 1-10 satisfaction scales.
- CSAT classification:
  - 1-5 scale: 4-5 Satisfied, 3 Neutral, 1-2 Dissatisfied
  - 1-7 scale: 6-7 Satisfied, 5 Neutral, 1-4 Dissatisfied
  - 1-10 scale: 8-10 Satisfied, 7 Neutral, 1-6 Dissatisfied
